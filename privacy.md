# Privacy Policy

*Last Updated: June 7, 2026*

This policy applies to all information collected or submitted on MAGmate's website, iOS app, iPad app, Apple Watch app, and widget extensions.

## Information We Collect

We do not collect personal information such as your name, email address, or phone number on our own servers. MAGmate does not require user accounts and operates without a backend service controlled by us.

## Local Network & Device Control

MAGmate uses your local network to discover and communicate with MAG set-top boxes on your Wi-Fi. This includes Bonjour service discovery and direct TCP connections to devices you add. These communications stay on your local network and are used only to send remote control commands and complete pairing. We do not route your remote commands through our servers.

## Data Stored on Your Device

The following information is stored locally on your Apple devices:

- Device names, IP addresses/hosts, and port numbers for MAG boxes you add
- Your selected active device and app preferences, such as haptic feedback settings
- Pairing credentials, stored securely in the iOS Keychain

Widget and Siri Shortcut features read this locally stored device information from a shared app container on your device so controls can work without opening the app.

## iCloud Sync (Optional)

If you enable iCloud Sync in Settings, your saved MAG device list and selected app preferences may be synced across your own Apple devices signed into the same iCloud account using Apple's NSUbiquitousKeyValueStore. Pairing credentials remain stored in the Keychain and are not synced through iCloud. We do not have access to your iCloud account or the data stored within it.

## Apple Watch Sync

If you use the Apple Watch companion app, device information is transferred between your iPhone and Apple Watch using Apple's WatchConnectivity framework so the watch remote can control your selected MAG box. This data stays within your own Apple devices.

## Analytics & Advertising

MAGmate does not use third-party analytics, advertising networks, or behavioral tracking services. We do not sell your data.

## Siri Shortcuts & System Integrations

MAGmate supports Apple system features such as App Intents, widgets, and Siri Shortcuts. When you use these features, Apple processes the request according to its own privacy policies. MAGmate only acts on commands you initiate.

## Support & Feedback

If you contact us by email or through the in-app feedback option, we receive the information you choose to send, such as your message and any debug details included by the app (for example, app version, device model, and iOS version). We use this only to respond to your request.

## Third-Party Services

MAGmate communicates directly with MAG set-top boxes using Infomir's remote control protocol over your local network. MAGmate is not affiliated with Infomir. Your use of a MAG device is subject to that hardware and firmware's own behavior and settings.

## Children's Privacy

MAGmate is not directed at children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last Updated" date at the top of this page will reflect the most recent revision.
